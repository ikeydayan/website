Knicks Basketball

Above - J.R. Smith makes an amazing dunk against the Cleveland Caviliers

The New York Knicks are a professional Basketball team in the NBA. They play in the Atlantic division in the Eastern Conference. Some famous players include Walt Frazier, Patrick Ewing, Charles Oakley, and more. They play in Madison Square Garden, known as "The World's Most Famous Arena." The Knicks were established in 1946. The Knicks are of two (2) teams of the original NBA still located in its original city. The Knicks have been struggling in the last few seasons while the last time they made the playoffs was in the 2012-2013 season.

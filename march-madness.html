March Madness

Above - A player gives his teammate a perfect alley oop

March Madness is the NCAA (College) basketball tournament that takes place every March. March Madness is a competitive and extremely intense tournament which makes it so great. It's mostly known for brackets people make to predict who will win each game and the championship. Some of the top seeds this year were Kansas, Villanova, Lousiville, Kentucky, and Duke. A great part about March Madness is that there are always upsets. The tournament lasts for around two (2) weeks until the championship game.

The Pittsburgh Steelers are a great NFL team. They play at Heinz Field in Pittsburgh, PA. They are in the the North division of the AFC Conference. In the history of the franchise, the team has won six (6) championships. The Steelers have one of the greatest fan bases in all of football.

Steelers Nation, view this site to see everything about the Steelers!

  • The Steelers have won 6 Super Bowls which is the most out of every team in the NFL
  • The Steelers are one of the oldest teams in the NFL, founded in 1933
  • The Steelers are one of the only teams to play in the same city since they were founded
  • The Rooney Family are the owners of the team
  • The colors of the Steelers are black and gold
